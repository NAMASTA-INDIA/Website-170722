About Us:

Kalam ka Sipahi is a collaborative initiative of Namaste India Foundation, Self Independent, TIP Community and many other organisations that aims to establish the world's first encyclopaedia of quotes and book summaries inspired by Deepak Ramola (Founder of Project Fuel) and his artistic & poetic work. We are welcoming volunteers to join hands in this revolutionary project and contribute with their skills. Send us an email at admin@kalamkasipahi.com and we will get in touch with you.
